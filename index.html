Welcome to my Website

This is Beauty Akter, student of Comilla University, department of Computer Science and Engineering (C.S.E). I am from Daudkandi, Comilla, in the division of Chittagong. As I come from nuclear family, except me, I have two elder brothers and my parents live in my family. My father, Md.Billal Hossen, is a businessman in his occupation and mother, Mrs. Nurjahan Begum, is a homemaker and two of my elder brothers live in abroad. I’m separated from my family for the last four years to complete my graduation from Comilla University.

From my childhood, I had a great dream to be a singer and also a teacher as well. Because, I think teaching is only the noble profession by which I can enkindle the light of knowledge to the poor children as I want to serve my countries people. I can sing well too and in my childhood one of my aunt had inspired me and told me that one day you will be a good singer. But now as I am a student of C.S.E, that’s why I think it would be better to be an engineer rather than a teacher. And I have a belief in God what is in store for me as he knows better than me. I just want to build up my career as an android developer in a well-known software company and improve myself as a programmer in future.
